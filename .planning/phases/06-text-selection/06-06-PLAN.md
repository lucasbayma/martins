<objective>
Wire the `App::clear_selection()` helper (created in Plan 01) into every tab-switch and workspace-switch site. Introduce `App::set_active_tab(idx)` as the canonical tab-switch primitive so bare `app.active_tab = N` writes are replaced by a single clearing helper. Extend `App::select_active_workspace` with a `clear_selection()` call. Add 2 unit tests proving both switches clear an active selection.

Purpose: D-22 says selection clears on tab switch and on workspace switch — the anchored generation is per-session, so cross-session highlight is meaningless. Without this plan, SelectionState would persist across tab switches and paint over the wrong cells on the new active session, or leak text into a cmd+c from a session the user is no longer viewing.

Output: 2 new / extended App methods, ~15 migrated call sites across `src/workspace.rs` and `src/events.rs`, 2 new unit tests.
</objective>
